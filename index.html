About Us

Matthew McCall

Matt (or as Michele calls him Mitchum Man) is from Frederick, MD, and got his degree from Salisbury University. After college, Matt spent his twenties living in Baltimore, where he developed a passion for crabcakes, ice hockey, improv theater, and christmas cards with cats on them. Matt met Michele through their mutual friends at a party in Baltimore, and eventually followed Michele out to LA.

Michele Romolini

Michele (or as Matt calls her Dr. Merd) is from Lansdowne, PA, and got her doctorate from University of Vermont. After undergrad, Michele was a dolphin trainer in Key Largo, FL before she went back to school. Michele was studying urban sustainability in Baltimore when she met Matt at a party. After grad school, she got a job at Loyola Marymount University in LA, where she now is a fellow and teacher.

Our Love Story

We First met

We met at a party hosted by our mutual friends, Angie and James. Prior to the party, Michele Facebook-stalked Matt thinking he had dating potential, and was unpleasantly surprised when he came in wearing highwater pants and man-clogs. However, she was eventually won over by his Nicolas Cage impersonations.

Dating

We started dating in Baltimore, but were both very busy at first; Matt was in a White House fellowship and Michele was finishing up her dissertation. As time went on, we started spending more and more time together, and discovered a mutual love of bicycling in the Baltimore Bike Party, traveling around the world together, and airport nachos.

Engagement

We got engaged on Christmas Eve, 2014 during a game of Cards against Humanity at Michele's sister's house. Matt had been waiting for the right time to propose, and secretly planning ideas with her sibilings. It finally felt like the perfect moment after Michele picked his card; which implied her cats were erotic lovers and should be in a broadway musical together.

Accommodation

Loews Santa Monica

First class views of the pier and awesome sunsets. We may book a small room block here or at the Marriott.

JW Marriott Santa Monica

Also a top class hotel similar to the Loews, with great views. We may book a small room block either here or at the Loews.

Le Meridien Delfina

Nice hotel a little inland, still very walkable to the wedding festivies with great views; in the Starwood Family.

Airbnb

If you are staying for an extended period, check out Airbnb. There are reasonable rates for homes in Santa Monica that can fit your needs. The earlier you book, the better.

Hotel MdR

Very Nice hotel in Marina Del Rey, recently remodeled with great amenities. Just a cheap Uber/Lyft/Taxi ride to all of the festivities.

Hilton MdR

Nice hotel in Marina Del Rey. Affordable taxi ride with plenty to walk to around it, or a short bike ride to the festivities.

Custom Hotel

Great Boutique Hotel with modern design. A little further out, so Uber/Lyft/Taxi or rental car is a must.

Hotel Erwin

Great Boutique Hotel with awesome views in the heart of Venice. Very walkable to all of the festivities. Only caveat, stay off the Venice beachfront after dark and stick to the neighborhoods instead.
